## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is SkoutLab?">
    SkoutLab is an AI-powered data analysis platform. Instead of building dashboards or writing queries, you connect your data and ask questions in plain language. Our AI agents explore your data, generate hypotheses, validate findings, and present actionable insights.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How is it different from traditional BI tools?">

  <Accordion title="Do I need technical skills?">

## Data & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is my data secure?">
    Yes. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256). Credentials are stored using industry-standard encryption. We never share your data with other users or use it to train models.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does SkoutLab modify my database?">
    No. SkoutLab is read-only. We query your data but never write, update, or delete anything in your source systems.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if I delete a connection?">
    All associated data, learnings, and analysis history are permanently deleted from our systems.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Where is data stored?">
    SkoutLab runs on secure cloud infrastructure. Data processing happens in isolated environments. Contact us for specific compliance questions.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Analysis

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does an analysis take?">
    Typically 2-5 minutes, depending on data size and question complexity. Simple questions on small datasets are faster; complex exploratory analyses take longer.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if the AI gets something wrong?">
    SkoutLab provides confidence levels and evidence for every finding. Always review the evidence before taking action. If you find errors, they help improve future analyses.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I ask follow-up questions?">
    Yes! Each finding has an "Investigate Further" option that creates a new focused analysis. You can iterate to dig deeper into specific areas.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does 'Confirmed' mean?">
    A confirmed hypothesis has passed statistical significance testing (p < 0.05 by default). This means there's less than 5% probability the pattern is due to random chance.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
