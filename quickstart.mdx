## Step 1: Connect Your Data

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Data Sources">
    Click **Data** in the sidebar to open the data management page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a Connection">
    Click **Add Connection** and choose your data source type:
    - **Upload Files** — Excel, CSV, or Parquet files
    - **Database** — Connect to PostgreSQL, MySQL, or other databases
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let AI Learn">
    Once connected, click **Start Learning**. Our AI will automatically explore your data structure, understand relationships, and build knowledge about your business.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
The learning process runs in the background. You can start asking questions immediately — the AI will get smarter over time.
</Info>

## Step 2: Ask Your First Question

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Task">
    Click **New Task** from the home page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Data">
    Choose which data sources to analyze.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask a Question">
    Type your question in plain language:
    - "What drove revenue changes last quarter?"
    - "Which products are underperforming?"
    - "Are there any anomalies in the data?"

    Or leave it blank — SkoutLab will explore and find interesting patterns automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Results">
    SkoutLab's AI agents will:
    1. Generate hypotheses about your data
    2. Test each hypothesis with statistical analysis
    3. Present findings with evidence and charts
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Review Your Report

Each analysis produces a report with:

| Section | What You'll See |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Key Findings** | Top insights ranked by business impact |
| **Evidence** | Charts, statistics, and data backing each finding |
| **Next Steps** | Recommended actions based on the analysis |

<Tip>
Click on any finding to see the detailed analysis, including the AI's reasoning process and supporting evidence.
</Tip>
